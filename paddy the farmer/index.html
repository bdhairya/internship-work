About Us

Paddy the Farmers is your favourite down-home, country pub.. in the city.
It's the kind of place where everbody knows your name. And if it's your
first time visiting, you feel welcomed and relaxed. It's the type of bar you
go to with friends and family when you want to have actual conversations,
or alone to watch the match or simply unwind. It's a bar - with live music
every Friday and Saturday night. But with surprisingly good food 'for a bar'
